Hi! My name is Kaede Takeshige.

I am studying animation at Brigham Young University, graduating April 2019. I am most passionate about modeling and rigging. I have been involved in three senior project films at my time at BYU, doing modeling, rigging, and animation.

Currently I am a team lead at BYU Independent Study, where we develop animated video content for online courses. I supervise 3D projects, asset creation, and some 2D After Effects work.

I was born in Japan, and grew up in Shanghai, China. I have lived in the US since 2013. I am 98.6% fluent in English, 70% in Japanese, and 50% in Chinese.
